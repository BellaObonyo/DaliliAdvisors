Empowering Livelihoods through Digital Advisory on Land, Livelihoods, crop and Livestock Insurance

Dalili Advisors is a pioneering consultancy firm specializing in the development and delivery of cutting-edge climate risk insurance solutions. We undertake feasibility assessments, design, pilot, and scale-up sustainable index insurance solutions targeting climate-vulnerable populations in Africa and beyond. Climate risk insurance is a proven solution towards mitigating against climate-related risks, such as droughts and excessive rainfall. Our approach anchors on combining climate risk insurance with other instruments to deliver innovative solutions towards

  • Building resilience to climate shocks
  • Transforming Agri-food systems
  • Financial inclusion
  • Mitigation of climate-induced conflicts
  • Climate Adaptation
