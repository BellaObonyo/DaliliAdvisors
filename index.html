Empowering Livelihoods through Digital Advisory on Land, Livelihoods, crop and Livestock Insurance

DALILI is a Swahili word for sign. At DALILI, we believe in interventions based on empirical evidence on occurrence of climate-induced disasters which would affect the economic base of marginalized households in the GHoA and the SAHEL. We leverage on the potential of Earth Observation (satellite data) and Big Data Analytics to monitor both ecological and climatic variables that affect the agricultural sector (livestock and crops) which are the economic mainstays of most sub-saharan African countries (SSA).

Land registry and cadastral mapping

Our team have technical background in survey including the use of Geotechnology (GIS, RS, photogrammetry, survey) in land resources information management..

Livelihoods advisory

Our team of financial advisors can help individuals and businesses develop a diversified income stream and build up emergency funds to protect against unexpected income disruptions.

Crop Insurance

We offer a range of crop insurance policies designed to help farmers mitigate the risks of weather events, pests, and other unforeseen circumstances..

Livestock Insurance

We offer a range of livestock insurance policies designed to help farmers protect against losses due to disease outbreaks, natural disasters, and other unexpected events..
